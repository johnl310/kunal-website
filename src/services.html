Frequently Asked Questions

What does the membership include?

Bi-weekly investment reports, weekly card picks across basketball and football, market trend analysis, rising and falling card alerts, card show calendars, exclusive discounts, monthly giveaway entries, and community access.

How does consignment through Fanatics Collect work?

Submit your card details and photos through our consignment form. Our team reviews your collection, handles secure logistics, professional promotion, and facilitates sales through Fanatics Collect with direct payouts to you.

What kind of returns can I expect?

Returns vary by market conditions and card selection. Members who followed Sports Card Invest recommendations have historically averaged around 60% monthly returns, though past performance does not guarantee future results.

Do I need experience to start?

No. The membership is designed for both beginners and experienced collectors. Reports include context, grading basics, and clear buy/sell signals so anyone can follow along.
