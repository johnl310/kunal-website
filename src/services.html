Frequently Asked Questions

How many picks do members get per week?

Members receive 23 in-depth card picks per week with full analysis across basketball, football, baseball, hockey, and soccer. 90% of this analysis is exclusive — it's not shared on YouTube or the free Instagram.

When are picks released?

Premium, Deluxe, Ultra High-End, and Platinum members receive picks via email every Sunday at 7PM EST. Monthly and yearly members receive picks via Instagram (@sportscardinvestmembers) on Mondays at 5PM EST.

How do I access member picks on Instagram?

After payment, message @sportscardinvest on Instagram with your order number. You'll be added to the private @sportscardinvestmembers account where picks are posted.

What's the difference between the tiers?

Deluxe ($199.99/yr) covers cards at all price points with 10 buy picks, 5-10 sell picks, and 3 underground NBA plays. Platinum ($695/yr) focuses on high-end $1K-$10K cards with monthly calls. Ultra High-End ($1,999/yr) covers $10K+ cards with access to all other tiers. Portfolio Management ($1,500) provides full consignment and strategic services.

Do I need experience to start?

No. Reports include full context, grading basics, and clear buy/sell signals. The Deluxe tier is the best entry point for beginners. More experienced collectors with $1K+ budgets per card should look at Platinum or Ultra High-End.
