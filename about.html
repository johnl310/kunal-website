About Kunal Ahuja

How It Started

I'm Kunal Ahuja — born and raised in the Bay Area. I've been passionate about sports my entire life, and during high school I developed an equal passion for business and entrepreneurship.

Before sports cards, I was flipping sneakers for quick cash. Then a friend pointed me to the sports card market — and I saw something bigger. The market was growing roughly 900% in eight months, and almost nobody was treating cards as a serious asset class.

I spent months building expertise — studying pricing data, tracking player performance, understanding grading and authentication through PSA, BGS, SGC, and CGC — before I ever launched anything publicly. That personal track record became the credibility foundation for everything that followed.

Building Sports Card Invest

I launched Sports Card Invest in 2019 at 18 years old, while still in college. The early days were pure grind — posting 8 to 10 times a day across platforms, often to almost no engagement. It took roughly 500 posts before things started to click.

Within three months, over 1,300 people paid to join the membership. The program went viral among investors because the research was real and the returns spoke for themselves — members averaged 60% monthly returns following the weekly reports.

During summer breaks, I was putting in 6 to 10 hour days — market research, content creation, order processing, community management — all at once. The work ethic never changed.

Where I Am Now

Today, Sports Card Invest is a full platform — 47K+ followers on Instagram (opens in a new tab), 1,500+ posts, and 23 in-depth card picks delivered to members every week. 90% of the analysis is exclusive to members — it never hits YouTube or the free channels.

The business has scaled into a multi-tier membership (Deluxe, Platinum, Ultra High-End) plus consignment services through Fanatics Collect (opens in a new tab), a YouTube channel (opens in a new tab), a podcast on Spotify (opens in a new tab), and an audiobook on Audible (opens in a new tab).

I cover basketball, football, baseball, hockey, and soccer — weekly buy/sell picks, market trend breakdowns, rising and falling card analysis, grading guidance, and card show event calendars.

My Investment Philosophy

Consistency Over Hype

500 posts before traction. 1,500+ posts later. The work compounds before the results do.

Data Over Gut

Every pick is backed by market data, player performance, and grading analysis — not hype cycles.

Portfolio Thinking

Cards are assets. Diversify across sports, price points, and grading tiers — just like any portfolio.

Stay Hungry

Markets shift. The edge belongs to whoever adapts fastest.
